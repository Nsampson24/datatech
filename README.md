<h3>MediTrack Global Health Operational Analysis</h3>
											<p>When I began this project, my purpose was clear: turn scattered healthcare data into a unified, meaningful story that leaders could use to improve care, strengthen operations, and protect resources. Coming from the healthcare world, I’ve seen firsthand how every number represents a real human moment — a patient fighting to recover, a nurse running a long shift, a billing officer trying to keep financials afloat. I wanted this dashboard to honor that reality. My goal was to build an analytical tool that connected three critical pillars: Financial Performance, Patient Insights, Operational Capacity, which could finally show MediTrack what was happening across ten countries, thousands of patients, and multiple departments — clearly, consistently, and in real time.</p>
